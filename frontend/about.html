Our Story

Welcome to Star King Power Tools

Your Trusted Source for Quality Tools and Spare Parts

Star King Power Tools and Spare Parts Private Limited, founded in 2014, has rapidly grown to become a premier provider of high-quality power tools, accessories, and spare parts in the industry. With a commitment to excellence and innovation, we serve a broad spectrum of industries including construction, manufacturing, woodworking, metalworking, automotive, and more.

Our extensive product range includes drills, saws, grinders, sanders, and a wide array of other power tools designed to meet the rigorous demands of both professionals and DIY enthusiasts.
